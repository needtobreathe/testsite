THE LEGEND OF HUEDINI

Huedini is a top-down action roguelike game. Play as Huedini, a powerful wizard with the ability to change his colour. By matching your colour, to that of the enemies you can absorb their colour essence. By combining these colour essences into the correct combination, you can cast custom spells.

Huedini is a top-down action roguelike game. You play as Huedini, a powerful wizard with the unique ability to change his colour. By matching your colour, to that of the enemies and their projectiles, you can absorb their colour essence to cast powerful spells. Collect upgrades as you progress to customise and improve your spells and abilities.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Why Wasn't It Released On Steam?

Our game is currently in the verification section on Steam. As you, our players, play our game and tell us your experiences, we forward them to Steam and they say we can or cannot release them. If things go as we think, the game will be released on Steam soon.

Why Our Experiences Are Necessary

The experience of our players is necessary because they are required to get approval from Steam and for our game to be released without any errors.

Will there be future DLC or updates?

We are committed to continuously expanding the world of Huedini. Regular updates will include new missions, characters, and gameplay features. A major expansion is planned for release within six months of launch.

How Many People in Your Team?

Our Team is 18 People, Counting Designers and Editors

How long is the main campaign?

The main campaign offers approximately 20-25 hours of gameplay. With side quests, collectibles, and additional content, players can expect 40+ hours of total gameplay experience.
